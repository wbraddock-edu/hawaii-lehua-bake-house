Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Lehua Bake House.

Home › FAQ
What time do you open?
We open at 6am seven days a week. Monday–Friday we close at 2pm, Saturday–Sunday at 3pm. We recommend arriving by 8–9am on weekends if you want the full pastry selection, as popular items like malasadas and almond croissants often sell out by 10am.
Can I pre-order pastries or cakes?
Yes! You can pre-order standard items (pastry boxes, malasada packs, croissant packs) through our online order form with same-day or next-day pickup. Custom cakes require at least 48 hours advance notice. For wedding cakes or large event orders, please contact us 2+ weeks in advance.
Where does your coffee come from?
All our coffee is sourced from farms on the Kona coffee belt — specifically from Holualoa and Kealakekua, within 20 miles of our bakery. Our coffee director Mele grew up on one of our partner farms. We offer single-origin Kona exclusively, roasted locally and never blended with cheaper beans.
Do you have gluten-free options?
We offer gluten-free muffins daily (two flavors) and gluten-free coffee cake on Fridays and Saturdays. All our coffee drinks, lilikoi lemonade, and coconut matcha are naturally gluten-free. Please note our kitchen does handle wheat flour, so there is a risk of cross-contamination for those with celiac disease.
Do you sell your Kona coffee beans to take home?
Yes! We carry 100% Kona coffee beans in 8oz and 16oz bags, available whole bean or ground. Roasted fresh weekly. These make exceptional gifts to bring home from the Big Island. We can also ship bags to the mainland — ask at the counter for details.
Do you have seating?
We have indoor seating for 16 and a small covered lanai with 8 additional seats overlooking Ali'i Drive. It gets busy on weekend mornings, so if you have a group of 4+, we recommend coming on a weekday or arriving early on weekends to secure your spot.
Can you do catering for events?
Yes! We offer breakfast catering for corporate events, weddings, and private gatherings. A typical catering order includes assorted pastry platters, coffee service, and fresh bread. For events of 20+ guests, we ask for at least one week advance notice. Contact us directly at (808) 555-0108 for catering inquiries.
What is your most popular item?
On the pastry side, it's a close race between the fresh malasadas (especially haupia cream-filled) and the almond croissant. For drinks, the single-origin Kona pour-over is our signature and what most people rave about. Our coffee scone is an underrated local favorite — we'd recommend it to anyone looking beyond the obvious choices.

More Questions?

Call us or stop by — we're always happy to chat over a cup of Kona.

Our Story

Paris-trained craft, Hawaiian heart, Kona roots.

Home › About
Founded 2018

A Love Letter to
the Kona Morning

Kalani Akana grew up in Kailua-Kona watching his mother bake sweet bread every Sunday morning. After training as a pastry chef in Paris and working in Honolulu hotels, he returned home with a dream: bring world-class pastry craft to the neighborhood he loved.

His wife Mele grew up on her family's coffee farm in Holualoa. She handles all coffee sourcing — selecting only the finest cherries from their farming neighbors on the Kona coffee belt.

"We wanted to create a place that felt like waking up at your favorite Hawaiian auntie's house. Coffee on the table, pastries warm from the oven, and no reason to rush."

Our Values

What Drives Our Baking

Local Sourcing

All coffee comes from farms within 20 miles. Most fruit ingredients — guava, lilikoi, banana — are sourced from Big Island farms.

Made From Scratch

Nothing here is from a mix or pre-made. Everything — doughs, fillings, glazes, syrups — is made by hand in our kitchen before dawn.

Community Hub

We're more than a bakery. We host farmers market popups, coffee cuppings, and baking workshops because we believe food brings community together.

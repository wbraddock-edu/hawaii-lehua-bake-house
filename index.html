Our Story

Where Kona Coffee
Meets Aloha Mornings

Lehua Bake House was opened in 2018 by Kalani and Mele Akana, a Kona couple who wanted their neighborhood to have a bakery worth waking up for. Kalani trained in pastry arts in Paris; Mele grew up on a coffee farm just above Kealakekua Bay.

Every cup of coffee is sourced within 20 miles. Every pastry is baked from scratch each morning. Every customer is welcomed like a neighbor — because in Kailua-Kona, they usually are.
